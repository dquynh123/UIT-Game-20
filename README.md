# 🎮 UIT: Guided by Memories  
*A UIT Story*

---

## 📌 Overview
**UIT: Guided by Memories** is a web-based narrative game developed for the UIT Dev Game competition, celebrating the 20th anniversary of the University of Information Technology (UIT).

The game follows a former UIT student who is mysteriously transported into a memory-based dimension of the campus. To return, the player must explore different buildings and complete challenges to recover fragmented memories across time.

---

## 🌟 Concept
The game combines **time-travel storytelling** with **interactive mini-games**, where each building represents a stage in UIT’s 20-year journey.

> “Thứ níu giữ chúng ta không phải là thời gian… mà là những ký ức.”

---

## 🕹️ Gameplay

### 🔄 Progression
Explore buildings in order:  
**E → A → B → D → C**

Each building unlocks after completing its mini-game.

---

### 🎯 Mini-games

- **Tòa E – Board Game**  
  Dice-based board game inspired by *Snake and Ladder*, featuring forward/backward mechanics.

- **Tòa A – Drag & Drop**  
  Sort books into correct categories (Reference, Textbook, Thesis).

- **Tòa B – Memory Game**  
  Flip cards to find matching pairs.

- **Tòa D – Typing Game**  
  Falling objects with letters; type correctly to eliminate them.

- **Tòa C – Quiz Game**  
  Answer questions related to UIT and its 20-year history.
